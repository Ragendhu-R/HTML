Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a famous scientist who developed the Theory of Relativity.

Timeline

  • 1879 - Born in Germany
  • 1905 - Published important scientific papers
  • 1915 - Developed General Relativity
  • 1921 - Won the Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1933 - Moved to the United States
  • 1955 - Died in Princeton, USA

Quote

"Imagination is more important than knowledge."
